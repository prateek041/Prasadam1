What is Prasadam ?

PRASADAM is a idea to provide food to all who can not afford three meals to them and their family. PRASADAM is the bridge to connect you to all and provide help to needy.

WE TAKE FOODS FROM HOUSES, RESTAURANTS, AND EVEN FROM THE CATERING SERVICES AND JOIN WITH NGOs TO PROVIDE FOOD TO ONE WHO LACK IN ABILITIES TO EARN FOOD FOR THEMSELVES
