India is at the second position in food wastage in whole world. In India we have lavish parties and lavish weddings, and everyone knows how much food is prepared in the functions, and how much is wasted.

A large amount of food is even wasted due to this.After a survey done by your team we found that the excessive food in parties and weddings is wasted as no one takes responsibility of it, neither the owner nor the catering team.

In PRASADAM we collect the extra food from the food services and houses and join with the local NGOs of the area to create food drives and distribute food in the slums and area with needy people.

We are connected to many local organisations who share our cause. we are looking forward to expand our connections so that we can provide food to as many as possible.

Our aim is not just to provide food but to provide qualtiy food. Food qualtiy check is prime necessity. We need to add more man power to provide necessary food standards.

This is not something that we can do alone, it is a community work, done by community for the community, so join our cause and help us to reach our goals.
